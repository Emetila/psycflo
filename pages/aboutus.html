About Us
At PSYCFLO

We believe that mental health support and menstrual well-being should be accessible to everyone, regardless of location, income, or societal stigma.

We are committed to breaking barriers by providing direct, stigma-free, and culturally inclusive support.

What We Do

AI-powered well-being platform

It is designed to provide direct access to essential mental health and menstrual aid services.

This will eliminate middlemen and ensure fast transparent support.

Vision and Mission Statements

Vision

PSYCFLO envisions a world where mental and menstrual health support is accessible, stigma-free, and inclusive for underserved communities. By combining advanced mental health tools with direct menstrual aid distribution, we empower individuals to seek help without financial or cultural barriers.

Mission

To provide affordable, anonymous, and accessible well-being support through USSD, SMS, WhatsApp, and mobile app solutions, ensuring that mental and menstrual health care reaches those who need it most.

Who We Help

We focus on low-income and rural communities, young adults, students, women and girls ensuring that no one is left behind.

Services We Offer

Mental Health Support

Via USSD, SMS, WhatsApp, AI chatbot and App

Menstrual aid

Request system

Safe community

Discussion rooms

Freemium counselling services

(3 free counselling/ therapy sessions, then paid plans).

Accessibility to all

Works even without smartphones, limited or no internet.

Why PSYCFLO?

Unlike traditional aid programs, we directly connect people to help, eliminating delays, reducing stigma, and making support available anytime, anywhere.
