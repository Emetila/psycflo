Privacy Policy

1.Introduction

At PSYCFLO, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

2.Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, and account.
  • credentials Health-Related Information: Responses to health-related inquiries.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with the platform, device details, and browsing activity.
  • Communications: Messages exchanged within the platform.
3.How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized support and services.
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.
  • Communicate important updates.
4.Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share data with:

  • Service providers assisting in platform operations.

5.Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

6.User Control Over Data

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal data.
  • Request data deletion.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
7.Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience. You can manage cookie settings through your device or browser.

8.Third-Party Links

PSYCFLO may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage users to review third-party policies.

9.Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

10.Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy Periodically. Continued use of PSYCFLO after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11.Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@psycflo.com.
